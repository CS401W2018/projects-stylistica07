Title and Topic of Your Site

Title: Filipino Cuisine Exploration

Topic: Showcasing Filipino dishes with recipes, cultural significance, and preparation techniques.

Why I Chose This Topic

Filipino cuisine is rich in flavor, culture, and history. As a Filipino, I want to share these unique dishes with others while also preserving my heritage. Creating this site allows me to combine my passion for cooking with web development skills, making it meaningful and educational.

Content of Each Page

  • Home Page: Introduction to Filipino cuisine, a welcome message, navigation menu links, and a featured dish of the week.
  • History of Filipino Cuisine: A timeline of Filipino food evolution, cultural influences, and native cooking techniques.
  • Recipes Page: Popular recipes with step-by-step instructions, images, and ingredient lists.
  • Dining Etiquette and Traditions: Overview of traditional customs, kamayan (eating with hands), and food's role in celebrations.
  • Featured Dish Table: A table listing 10 dishes with fields for dish name, ingredients, origin, cooking time, and spiciness level.
  • Feedback Page: A form collecting name, email, favorite dish, and comments.

Information Collection Plan

Information for the site: Content from personal knowledge, family recipes, and cultural insights. Additional data from food blogs and cookbooks.

Images: Personal photos or royalty-free images from sites like Unsplash or Pexels.

Acknowledgment Statement
