Engagemen Questions

  • The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do? -The "action" attribute in the
    tag specifies the URL where the form's data will be sent when submitted.

  • The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do? -The "method" attribute defines how the form data will be sent to the server, either by GET or POST.

  • What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?/li> - "POST" is used to send data to a server, while "GET" is used to request data from a server.
