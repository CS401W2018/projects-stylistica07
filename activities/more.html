Understanding the Box Model

This section explains the box model concepts such as padding, border, and margin.

Answers to Engagement Questions

  1. Block tags vs. inline tags: Block tags occupy the entire width and start on a new line, while inline tags only take up as much space as needed and remain on the same line.
  2. Margin, border, and padding: Margin is the outer space, the border is the edge, and padding is the inner space between the content and border.
  3. Units of measurement in CSS: CSS units include px (pixels), % (percent), em, rem, vw, and vh.
  4. When to use a figure tag: Use 'figure' for content like images or diagrams that require a caption.
