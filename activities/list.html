What are special entity characters?

Special entity characters are reserved characters that are used to print out characters that cannot be found on a standard keyboard.

example:

1 < 2

3 > 2

What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

"OL" tag is used to create a list where the order of item matters, while a "UL" tag is used for a list where the order of item is not important or displayed with bullet points.

Example of Unordered list

My favorite colors

  • yellow
  • green
  • white
  • red
