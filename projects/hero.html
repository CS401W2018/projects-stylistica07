My Cooking Hero: Ninong Ry

Interests Page Hero Page

Ninong Ry is a popular Filipino vlogger chef who has gained a massive following for his entertaining cooking videos. He is known for his humorous personality, and his ability to make Filipino dishes both fun and accessible to a wide audience. Ninong Ry's passion for Filipino cuisine, combined with his casual and humble style, has made him a favorite among food lovers in the Philippines and abroad.

Popular Dishes
Lechon Kawali, Sinigang, Adobo
Notable Achievements
Millions of subscribers on YouTube, guest appearances on cooking shows
Social Media
YouTube, Facebook, Instagram
