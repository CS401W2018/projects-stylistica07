About the Pokémon Data

This table provides information about a few popular Pokémon, including their names, types, and levels.

Pokémon are creatures from the world-famous Pokémon franchise, where trainers catch, train, and battle Pokémon. Each Pokémon has a specific type, such as Electric, Fire, Water, or Grass, which determines its strengths and weaknesses in battles.

The levels represent each Pokémon's experience and strength. As they level up, they become more powerful and may learn new moves or even evolve into stronger forms.
